About Me

I hold a degree in Statistical Engineering from Universidad de Valparaíso and a Master's degree in Philosophy of Science from Universidad de Santiago de Chile (USACH).

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Statistics, where my research focuses on causal inference, multivariate methods, and the use of generalized additive models under the Rubin Causal Model framework.

My academic formation combines rigorous training in both applied statistical modeling and the philosophical foundations of science, enabling a multidisciplinary approach to research and teaching.
