About Me

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Statistics, focusing on causal inference, multivariate methods, and generalized additive models under the Rubin Causal Model framework.

I hold a degree in Statistical Engineering from Universidad de Valparaíso and a Master's in Philosophy of Science from USACH. My work bridges the gap between rigorous statistical modeling and the philosophical foundations of science.
